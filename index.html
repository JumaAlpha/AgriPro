What Is the BOOST Project?

The BOOST Project is an EU-supported initiative designed to strengthen community resilience, climate action, youth employment, and sustainable food systems in rural areas across Kenya and the region.

"BOOST" focuses on building capacity, supporting innovation, and scaling successful community models that protect the environment and improve livelihoods.
