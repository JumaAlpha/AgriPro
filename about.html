About Agri-Pro Solutions

Building a model of hope in Homabay County through agro-ecology, youth empowerment, and climate resilience.

Our Story

In Homabay County, smallholder farmers face the daily realities of climate change: erratic rains, depleted soils, high energy costs, and shrinking opportunities for youth. Yet, from this challenge, Agri-Pro Solutions CBO has built a model of hope—one that is restoring the land, empowering young people, and creating climate-smart livelihoods.

Led by committed youths drawn from across the sub-county under the chairmanship of Mr. Ben Blasto Nyagol, our community-based organization places agro-ecology, farm produce aggregation & marketing, clean energy, and innovation at the heart of rural transformation.

Today, our integrated approach is reaching across Homabay County, directly improving the lives of thousands of families through sustainable agricultural practices and community empowerment.

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by our core purpose and aspirations for the future

Our Mission

To empower rural communities in Rachuonyo North through integrated agro-ecological practices, clean energy solutions, and youth entrepreneurship, building climate resilience and sustainable livelihoods while restoring environmental health.

Our Vision

A transformed Rachuonyo North where thriving rural communities practice climate-smart agriculture, youth are empowered as agri-preneurs, and sustainable ecosystems support prosperous livelihoods for generations to come.

In Action

Our mission comes to life through hands-on training, community engagement, and sustainable agricultural practices that transform lives and landscapes.
