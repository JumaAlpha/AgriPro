Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our work

How can I partner with Agri-Pro Solutions?

We welcome partnerships with organizations, institutions, and corporations. You can support through funding, technical assistance, equipment donation, or program collaboration. Use the partnership form on our Get Involved page or contact us directly.

Can I visit your demonstration plots?

Yes! We welcome visitors to our demonstration plots, aquaculture sites, and BSF production unit. Please schedule your visit in advance using the contact form so we can arrange for a guide and ensure someone is available to show you around.

Do you offer training for farmers?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training in agro-ecology, aquaculture, BSF production, and irrigation technology. We've trained over 2,500 farmers and 300 youth. Contact us to learn about upcoming training sessions or to arrange group training.

How can local youth get involved?

Youth can participate in our training programs, start agribusinesses with our support, join our youth empowerment programs, or volunteer. We specifically focus on creating opportunities for young people in sustainable agriculture and green entrepreneurship.
