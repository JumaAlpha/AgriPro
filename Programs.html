Our Integrated Approach

We address the interconnected challenges of climate change, food security, and youth unemployment through eight complementary programs

Agro-ecology & Climate-Resilient Farming

Building farmer resilience through sustainable practices that work with nature rather than against it.

Key Achievements

  • Trained 40 Agro-ecological Service Providers (ASPs) as trainers of farmers
  • 2,500+ smallholder farmers trained in sustainable practices
  • 30,000+ tree seedlings distributed for soil health and income

Practices Taught

  • Soil fertility management (composting, mulching)
  • Crop diversification & indigenous seeds
  • Integrated pest management
  • Agroforestry & water conservation

EU Value Proposition: Scaling proven, environmentally sustainable agricultural practices

Aquaculture Department

Sustainable fish farming providing protein-rich food and income opportunities for the community.

Fish Species

  • Tilapia - Fast-growing, popular species
  • Catfish - Hardy species suitable for local conditions
  • Both species raised at Kendu Bay showground

Benefits

  • Diversified protein sources for local communities
  • Income generation through fish sales
  • Youth employment in aquaculture
  • Sustainable water resource utilization

Waste to Wealth: Black Soldier Fly Innovation

Transforming organic waste into high-value animal feed through innovative Black Soldier Fly (BSF) production.

Current Impact

  • 100+ farmers and youth trained in BSF production
  • Developing a BSF-Based Feed Formulation Center
  • Creating youth-led enterprises in circular economy

Future Goals

  • Produce affordable, sustainable animal feed
  • Scale circular economy model across the region
  • Create additional income streams for youth

EU Value Proposition: Investing in innovative, circular economy solutions that reduce environmental impact and create green jobs

Irrigation & Food Security for Economic Resilience

Enabling farming beyond rainfall patterns for year-round food production and income generation.

Partnership with KickStart International

  • Distributed 80+ Money-Maker irrigation pumps to farmers
  • Enabled dry-season vegetable and fruit production
  • Significantly reduced crop failure rates

Results Achieved

  • Diversified incomes for smallholder farmers
  • Improved year-round food security
  • Enhanced resilience to climate variability

EU Value Proposition: Building climate adaptation capabilities at the household level, directly mitigating the effects of drought
